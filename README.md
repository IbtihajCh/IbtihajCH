## 🧩 Blocksmith — The Main Thing

&lt;p align="center"&gt;
  &lt;a href="https://cut-boar-0a3.notion.site/Blocksmith-Multiview-Sparse-Voxel-Reconstruction-51de988c3cbb4e00a8922d8d8d519c35"&gt;
    &lt;img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/🔥_Blocksmith-Multiview_Sparse_Voxel_Reconstruction-00D4AA?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=0d1117" /&gt;
  &lt;/a&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;

**Blocksmith** is my ongoing research into **multiview sparse voxel reconstruction** — essentially, can we build rich 3D scenes from a handful of images without melting the GPU?

I started this because I was frustrated with how most neural reconstruction methods either need dense views or infinite VRAM. I'm exploring **sparse voxel octrees** married with **neural implicit surfaces** (think S-VolSDF, VolSDF territory) to make 3D reconstruction practical for real-world sparse inputs.

**What I'm solving:**
- 🧊 Memory-efficient geometry encoding via sparse voxels
- 👁️ Novel view synthesis from highly sparse multi-view inputs  
- ⚡ Making neural volume rendering feasible on consumer hardware

**The stack:** PyTorch, CUDA, custom ray-marching, implicit SDF networks, and a lot of patience.

&gt; [Read the research notes on Notion](https://cut-boar-0a3.notion.site/Blocksmith-Multiview-Sparse-Voxel-Reconstruction-51de988c3cbb4e00a8922d8d8d519c35)

## 🧪 My Research Playground

I don't just build apps — I experiment with how machines perceive language, images, and 3D space.

### 🏗️ 3D Vision & Geometry
The deep end. Neural radiance fields, implicit surfaces, voxel representations, and differentiable rendering. Blocksmith lives here.

### 🧠 NLP & Behavioral ML
Text is just another signal. I've trained models to predict personality types from writing ([MBTI Predictor](https://github.com/IbtihajCh/MBTI-Personality-Predictor)), analyze sentiment at scale ([Amazon Reviews](https://github.com/IbtihajCh/Sentiment-Analysis-on-Amazon-Reviews)), and optimize resumes for ATS systems. All deployed and live.

### ⚡ ML Systems & Inference
Models are useless if they don't run fast. I've benchmarked REST vs tRPC vs gRPC for AI inference, built microservices architectures for model serving, and obsessed over latency vs payload tradeoffs so you don't have to.
